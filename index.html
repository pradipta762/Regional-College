About Us

Welcome to Regional Group Of Institutions! we're a leading educational institution in Nimapada, Odisha. With experienced faculty and modern facilities, we offer quality education in +2 Science and +3 Science. Our spacious campus, well-equipped labs, and supportive environment foster holistic growth and prepare students for success in their chosen fields. Join us today, embark on a journey of academic excellence, and unlock your potential for a brighter future.
