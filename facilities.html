Basic Facilities

  • State-of-the-art laboratory facilities with well-maintained equipment.
  • A well-established library with a diverse collection of academic resources.
  • Separate hostel accommodations available for male and female students.
  • Highly experienced and qualified faculty members possessing both academic and industry backgrounds.
  • Provision of free uniforms and books to students.
  • On-campus cafeteria facilities for the convenience of students.
  • Career counseling services to guide students in making informed academic and professional decisions.
  • 24x7 healthcare services and attention to ensure the well-being of students.

Unique Facilities

  • Scholarship opportunities available for deserving, meritorious, and SC/ST students.
  • Organized study tours tailored for professional development.
  • Regular seminars and workshops conducted to enhance academic and practical knowledge.
  • Motivational and inspirational sessions aimed at fostering personal and professional growth.
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage provided for each student, ensuring their well-being.
