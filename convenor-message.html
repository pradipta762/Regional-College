Message from Convenor

Dear Alumni,

It brings me immense pleasure to extend a warm welcome to all our esteemed members of the Regional Group Of Institutions alumni community. As the Alumni Convenor, I am deeply honored to be entrusted with the responsibility of fostering connections and strengthening the bonds that tie us together.

Our alumni are the heart and soul of our institution, and your achievements are a testament to the quality education and values instilled in you during your time here. Whether you are excelling in your chosen field, making a difference in your community, or pursuing further education, your success is a source of pride for all of us at Regional Group Of Institutions.

As we look back on our shared history and reminisce about the memories we created together, let us also look towards the future with optimism and enthusiasm.

I encourage each and every one of you to stay connected and engaged with our alumni network. Whether it's attending alumni events, mentoring current students, or sharing your expertise and experiences, there are countless ways to give back to your alma mater and make a positive impact on the next generation.

Together, let us celebrate our achievements, support one another in our endeavors, and continue to uphold the values that define us as members of the Regional Group Of Institutions family. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to seeing the incredible contributions our alumni will continue to make in the years to come.
